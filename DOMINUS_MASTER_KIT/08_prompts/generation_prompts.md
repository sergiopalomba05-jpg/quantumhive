# Prompts de generacion

Usar estos prompts como base, ajustando solo pose, encuadre y contexto.

## Prompt base Dominus

```text
Dominus, official persistent presenter avatar for QuantumHive, premium human android executive, same face identity, long dark hair styled back and slightly to the side, confident serious expression, elegant black biomechanical suit with subtle gold technology details, no readable text on the suit, no logo letters, luxury technology aesthetic, dark premium lighting, realistic cinematic style, sharp face, consistent identity, transparent background
```

## Negative prompt

```text
different face, different hairstyle, cartoon, mascot, bee character, exaggerated robot, cheap armor, readable logo text on suit, distorted hands, extra fingers, childish style, low quality, blurry, inconsistent identity
```
